# Unity Version

As of February 2026, the properties of Risk of Rain 2's exe indicate a File Version of [2021.3.33.23082](https://unity.com/releases/editor/whats-new/2021.3.33#installs)

The Product Version field reads as "2021.3.33f1 (ee5a2aa03ab2)"

Note: A significant security vulnerability CVE-2025-59489 was identified in September 2025 affecting nearly all Unity games built from 2017 onwards.

**If you are using older version of Risk of Rain 2 from before September 12 2025, be aware of the potential security risk. **

On September 11th 2025 at 8:04:50 AM, Unity released an updated unityplayer.dll, according to its digital file signature.
September 11th 2025 at 10:34:38 PM is the timestamp of Risk of Rain 2's current digital file signature. 

Steam released a [new Client update](https://steamcommunity.com/groups/steamworks/announcements/detail/524229329545071275) that blocks the launching of custom URI schemes to prevent exploitation through its distribution platform.
[Microsoft has their own support article.](https://msrc.microsoft.com/update-guide/vulnerability/CVE-2025-59489)
